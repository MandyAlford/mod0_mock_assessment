Make a sandwich

1. get pantry ingredients
  1.1 open pantry door
  1.2 get bread
  1.3 get salt
  1.4 get pepper
  1.5 close pantry door
  1.6 carry pantry ingredients to counter
2. get refrigerated ingredients
  2.1 open refrigerator door
  2.2 get hummus
  2.3 get avocado
  2.4 get tomato
  2.5 close refrigerator door
  2.6 carry refrigerated ingredients to counter
3. get plate
  3.1 open cabinet door
  3.2 remove plate
  3.3 close cabinet door
  3.4 carry plate to counter
4. get silverware
  4.1 open silverware drawer
  4.2 get butter knife
  4.3 get vegetable slicing knife
  4.4 get spoon
  4.5 close silverware drawer
  4.6 carry silverware to counter
5. add bread
  5.1 open bread bag
  5.2 pull out 2 slices of bread
  5.3 place bread slices on plate
6. add hummus
  6.1 open hummus container
  6.2 insert butter knife into hummus container
  6.3 scoop out hummus on knife
  6.4 spread hummus on bread
7. add avocado
    7.1 slice open avocado with vegetable slicing knife
    7.2 scoop out avocado flesh with spoon
    7.3 place avocado flesh on bread
8. add tomato slices
    8.1 slice tomato with vegetable slicing knife 3 times
    8.2 place each tomato slice on bread
9. add salt
    9.1 hold salt shaker over bread
    9.2 turn salt shaker upside down
    9.3 shake salt shaker twice
10. add pepper
    10.1 hold pepper shaker over bread
    10.2 turn pepper shaker upside down
    10.3 shake pepper shaker 4 times
11. press bread slices together, ingredients facing inward
